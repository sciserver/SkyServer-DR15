The Right Search

Here are the magic words that let you search for all bright blue galaxies:

select top 10 p.objid, p.ra, p.dec, p.g, p.r
from photoObj p
join specObj s on s.bestobjid = p.objid
where p.g-p.r < 0
and p.g < 15
and p.r < 15
and s.class = 2

Don't worry, you'll learn what each of these terms means later in the tutorial.
