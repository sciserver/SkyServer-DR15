SpecObj

This table contains all information on spectra measured by the SDSS. The list below shows the columns most commonly searched. For a complete list, see the Schema Browser .

specObjID unique ID for the spectrum
fiberID fiber number (used in the Get Spectrum tool)
z redshift
zErr error in redshift
zWarning a flag marking any errors in the redshift measurement for this spectrum; a value of 0 means the redshift is reliable
class spectral classification ['GALAXY', 'QSO', or 'STAR']
ra right ascension of object
dec declination of object
fiberMag_u fiber magnitude (object's magnitude seen through the hole where the spectral fiber is plugged) in u
fiberMag_g fiber magnitude (object's magnitude seen through the hole where the spectral fiber is plugged) in g
fiberMag_r fiber magnitude (object's magnitude seen through the hole where the spectral fiber is plugged)in r
fiberMag_i fiber magnitude (object's magnitude seen through the hole where the spectral fiber is plugged) in i
fiberMag_z fiber magnitude (object's magnitude seen through the hole where the spectral fiber is plugged) in z
bestObjID photo ID of object as of the final data processing; bestObjID corresponds to "ObjID" in the PhotoObj table.
plateID unique ID of the plate on which this object was observed, also stored in the plate table
